Onsite Testing Facility

At Energy Alternatives, we understand the importance of reliable power solutions. That’s why we offer a state-of-the-art on-site testing facility to ensure the performance, durability, and safety of your power generation equipment. Our onsite testing facility is a comprehensive service designed to evaluate the functionality and performance of power generators and related equipment at your location, minimizing downtime and providing accurate insights into the condition of your equipment.

Key Features and Benefits:

  • Comprehensive Testing: Equipped with advanced tools to assess every aspect of your generator’s performance including load testing, voltage regulation, and fuel consumption.
  • Customized Solutions: Tailored testing procedures to meet your specific needs and address any concerns.
  • On-site Convenience: Testing conducted directly at your location eliminates the need for equipment transportation, saving time and reducing logistics complexity.
  • Minimized Downtime: Efficient testing minimizes disruption to your operations while ensuring equipment functionality.
  • Expert Analysis: Detailed reports and actionable recommendations provided after testing to enhance performance and resolve any issues.

Our Testing Process

Our testing process is carefully designed to provide thorough analysis and actionable results while minimizing disruptions to your operations.

  • Preparation: We work with you to schedule testing at a convenient time, ensuring minimal impact on operations.
  • Testing Execution: Using advanced testing equipment, we assess the performance, efficiency, and safety of your power generation systems.
  • Analysis and Reporting: Our team analyzes the results and provides a detailed report with findings, recommendations, and next steps for maintenance or improvements.
  • Follow-up Support: We provide continuous support after testing to address any concerns or implement recommendations.

Testing & Auditing Services for Solar & Electrical

Our Testing and Auditing Services for solar and electrical systems are designed to ensure optimal performance, safety, and compliance. Our team conducts thorough inspections and assessments to identify potential issues, evaluate system efficiency, and verify adherence to industry standards.

Key Testing Equipment:

  • Micro Test 5034: Used for detailed solar and electrical system testing.
  • Solar 300: Provides solar irradiance measurements and electrical performance testing.
  • Tri KA: An advanced tool for measuring current and voltage in electrical systems.
  • Solar Kable: Ensures proper wiring and connectivity in solar installations.
  • HT-M75: A multifunction meter for testing solar installations.
  • Thermal Imager: Identifies hotspots or potential faults through thermal imaging of electrical components.
  • Earth Meter: Used to test the grounding of electrical systems for safety and compliance.
  • Multi Meter: A versatile tool for measuring various electrical parameters, including voltage, current, and resistance.

By using cutting-edge testing equipment and auditing techniques, we provide actionable insights to enhance the reliability, safety, and performance of your solar and electrical infrastructure.
